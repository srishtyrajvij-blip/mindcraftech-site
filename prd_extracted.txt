This PRD outlines all content, structure, and functional requirements for the mindcraft.ai website. The site will position mindcraft.ai as a leading AI transformation partner for businesses, offering end-to-end artificial intelligence services — from strategic consulting to production-grade deployments. All copy has been crafted for SEO performance, clarity, and conversion.

7. SEO Strategy Requirements
7.1 Global SEO Principles
- All pages must include a unique `<title>` tag (50–60 characters) and `<meta description>` (150–160 characters)
H1 tag: one per page, containing the primary keyword
H2/H3 tags: used for logical content hierarchy, incorporating secondary keywords naturally
Internal linking: every service page links to at least 2 other relevant service or industry pages
Image alt tags: all images must include descriptive, keyword-aware alt text
Schema markup: implement Organization, Service, FAQPage, and BreadcrumbList structured data where applicable
Page URLs: short, descriptive, lowercase-hyphenated slugs (no query parameters)
Canonical tags: all pages must include a self-referencing canonical tag
XML sitemap and robots.txt must be configured on launch
